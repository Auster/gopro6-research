### gpauto Examples

## How to use gpauto

1) Copy gpauto in SD card root
2) Replace `Password` to your password
3) Remove battery
4) Insert SD card
5) Insert battery
6) Wait at least 10 seconds
7) Remove battery
8) Wait at least 2 seconds
9) Insert battery
